DAFT PUNK

THEY WILL RULE THE NATION

If you say you never heard of daft punk you're either a liar or a fool. The world famous french duo turned the electronic scene upside down in the 90's. Their legendary debut album 'homework' is still considered as one of the greatest of all time in the scene. In 2013 they released a new album including the worldwide super succeshit 'get lucky' with pharell. So if you come to OCEAN festival this summer, you'll beter come prepared because daft punk will deliver an expierance you never had before.
